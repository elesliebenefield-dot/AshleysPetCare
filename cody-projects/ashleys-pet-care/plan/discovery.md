# Discovery
This document captures the raw, unfiltered idea or initial AI prompt that sparked the project.

## Starting Prompt
A modern, mobile-first website for Ashley's Pet Care, a pet sitting and dog walking business serving Lake Nona and Greater Orlando. The website should build trust, showcase Ashley's experience and personality, use real photography, and generate inquiries from pet owners seeking dog walking, pet sitting, drop-in visits, medication administration, and personalized pet care.

## Questions

**AGENT Q1:** How do you want people to contact Ashley — is the goal a simple inquiry form, a link to an existing booking tool, or something else?
**USER A1:** A simple inquiry form with name, contact information, pet information, requested service, and message. Ashley will respond personally to discuss availability and scheduling.

**AGENT Q2:** Does Ashley have any existing branding — a logo, preferred colors, or a style she has in mind?
**USER A2:** No existing logo. Brand direction: modern, warm, trustworthy, community-focused. Boutique pet care feel — not childish or cartoonish. Real photography central. Favorite color is Barbie pink, used as a tasteful rose-pink accent. Palette: warm cream background, deep charcoal text, soft sage green, rose-pink accents. Logo: clean wordmark "Ashley's Pet Care" with tagline "From Leash to Love." Possible icon: leash forming a heart, subtle paw/heart, or clean dog/cat line-art.

**AGENT Q3:** What platform or tech stack should the site be built on, and who will maintain it?
**USER A3:** Next.js with React and TypeScript. Developer maintains the codebase. Ashley can update basic content (photos, service descriptions, contact info) with assistance.

**AGENT Q4:** What is Ashley currently doing to get clients, and are there any timeline or budget constraints?
**USER A4:** Currently gets clients through Rover and word of mouth. The site should mention Rover as a trust signal but the goal is a professional web presence outside of Rover. No strict launch deadline — build a polished first version. Budget should stay low; avoid paid third-party tools unless truly necessary. No online payments or booking system for V1.

**AGENT Q5:** What does success look like, and what features would you want in a future version?
**USER A5:** Success = pet owners find Ashley online, feel confident and comfortable, and reach out directly without needing Rover. Primary metrics: consistent inquiries from local pet owners, increased visibility outside Rover, strong first impression and credibility, converting visitors into meet-and-greet requests. Future features: photo gallery, testimonials/reviews, service area map, FAQ, blog/pet care tips, online booking, social media integration.

**AGENT Q6 (follow-up):** Please share more about Ashley's background, experience, and the tone you want the site to convey.
**USER A6:** Ashley is a wife, mother, dog mom, and experienced cat owner. She has lived in Central Florida for nearly 30 years and is active in the community, including volunteering with Scouts of America. She has experience with senior pets, rescue dogs, medication administration, pets recovering from injuries, mobility-challenged pets, and picky eaters (including hand feeding). She cares for dogs, cats, puppies, and small companion animals. Tone: pets are treated as family members, care is personalized to each pet's individual needs.

## Discovery Summary

**Ashley's Pet Care** is a pet sitting and dog walking business run by Ashley, a deeply experienced, community-rooted caregiver based in Central Florida. This project is a modern, mobile-first website built with Next.js, React, and TypeScript that serves as Ashley's independent professional web presence — separate from Rover, where she currently receives most of her clients.

**The problem:** Ashley has genuine expertise, a warm personality, and deep community roots, but her professional identity lives on a third-party marketplace. Pet owners who find her on Rover have limited ability to understand who she really is. The website gives Ashley full ownership of her story, her brand, and her first impression.

**The audience:** Pet owners in Lake Nona and Greater Orlando who want more than a transactional service — they want someone they can trust to treat their pet as a member of the family, not just another appointment.

**Who Ashley is:** A wife, mother, dog mom, and experienced cat owner with nearly 30 years in Central Florida. She volunteers with Scouts of America and is active in her local community. Her experience spans senior pets, rescue dogs, medication administration, injury recovery, mobility-challenged pets, picky eaters (including hand feeding), puppies, cats, and small companion animals.

**Brand:** Built from scratch. Warm, modern, boutique — not childish or cartoonish. Palette of warm cream, deep charcoal, soft sage green, and rose-pink accents. Logo: "Ashley's Pet Care" wordmark with tagline *"From Leash to Love."*

**Version 1 scope:**
- Hero section with real photography and tagline
- About Ashley — personality, experience, community ties
- Services — dog walking, pet sitting, drop-in visits, medication administration, personalized care
- Rover mentioned as a trust signal
- Inquiry form — name, contact info, pet info, requested service, message
- Mobile-first, responsive design
- No paid tools, no booking system, no payments

**Success metrics:** Consistent direct inquiries from local pet owners, increased visibility outside Rover, strong first impressions, meet-and-greet conversions.

**Future versions:** Photo gallery, testimonials, service area map, FAQ, blog, booking integration, social media.
